## Key Findings for Manuscript

### Headline results
1. **57.6% of Canadian mental health pharmacotherapy RCTs report race/ethnicity** — 42.4% do not
2. **0% conduct intersectional analysis** — absolute zero across all 66 trials
3. **6.1% explicitly distinguish sex from gender** (4 trials)
4. **3.0% report any SOGI data** (2 trials)
5. **0% report religion** — complete absence
6. **0% reference OCAP or any Indigenous data governance framework** — despite 7 trials documenting Indigenous participants (up to 21.7% in one trial)
7. **72.7% have no CONSORT-Equity compliance**

### Funder patterns
- **50% of CIHR-funded trials** (6 of ~12) do not report race/ethnicity
- NIH-funded trials more consistently report race (driven by NIH inclusion reporting mandate)
- Industry-funded trials vary widely

### Temporal patterns (visual inspection)
- Trials published 2016-2019: lower race reporting rates
- Post-2020 trials show modest improvement, with sex/gender distinction emerging only in 2022+
- All 4 sex/gender-distinguishing studies published 2022-2025

### Best and worst EDIA reporting
- **Best:** Record #79 (OPTIMA, Jutras-Aswad 2022) — 10 race categories, First Nations/Metis named, sex/gender distinguished, transgender data, income in CAD, education, drug coverage, living stability
- **Worst gaps:** Record #102 (PGx trial calibrated for racial groups but doesn't collect race), Record #132 (largest trial without race, n=354)

**QC coverage limitation:** The extraction codebook specifies 7 fields for inter-rater reliability testing. Pass 2 QC assessed 4 of these 7 fields (race_reported, sex_gender_distinguished, indigenous_participation, education_reported). Three fields were not independently verified: indigenous_data_sovereignty, ses_reported, and consort_equity_compliant. The 4 tested fields include the primary outcome (race_reported) and two high-priority secondary variables. The untested fields have lower expected variance (indigenous_data_sovereignty is 0 for all 63 included studies; ses_reported and consort_equity_compliant have straightforward coding rules). This partial coverage is acknowledged as a limitation.
