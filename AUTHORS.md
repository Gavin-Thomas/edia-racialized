## Contributions

All stages of this scoping review — research question development, protocol
drafting, search strategy design, database searching, deduplication, title/abstract
screening (AI-assisted), full-text screening, and documentation — were performed
by the author with AI-assisted tooling (LLM agents for screening, as disclosed in
the root README).

Data extraction is complete as of 2026-04-13 (63 studies, Pass 2 QC done, all kappa > 0.86). Manuscript drafting is in progress.
